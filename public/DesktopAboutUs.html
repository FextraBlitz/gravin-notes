About Us

Re-imagining Productivity with AI and Simplicity

Who We Are

We're a team of students, creators, builders, and thinkers passionate about helping people work smarter—not harder. We believe that great ideas deserve a space where they can grow, evolve, and take shape without distraction.
That’s why we built GRAVIN Notes—a modern workspace that combines intuitive note-taking with the power of AI, helping you capture, organize, and understand your thoughts with clarity.

Our Mission

We’re here to help individuals stay focused, informed, and in control of their ideas by giving them tools that are intelligent, simple, and flexible.

What We Do

  • Smart Text Editors – Designed for clarity, focus, and freedom of expression
  • AI Summarizer Assistant – A conversational tool that helps you summarize, explore, and interact with your content
  • Unified Workspace – A seamless experience where note-taking meets AI-powered understanding

What We Do

We saw the gap between traditional note-taking tools and the growing need for deeper understanding. Long notes pile up. Important details get lost. And traditional tools can’t keep up. So, we created GRAVIN Notes—where your ideas don’t just get stored, they get smarter.
